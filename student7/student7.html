Animal Crossing

Animal Crossing is a game on the DS or 3DS. You plsy as a new person coming to a village full of animals. At the begining, a man asks you questions to determain your avatar. You can't choose your avatar.

The place that you move to has alot of animal that are working different jobs at different shops. You can do things at certain shops such as mail letters and items to your friends, buy items to decorate your shop,and travel to other villages.
